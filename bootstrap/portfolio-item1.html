CompTIA A+ and TestOut PC Pro certificates

Project Description

Successfully took the two tests required to become CompTIA A+ certified and took and passed the performance-based TestOut PC Pro certification exam.

Project Details

  • Completed the TestOut PC Pro course and took the certification exam
  • Took and passed the CompTIA A+ 220-901 and 220-902 exams

This project was taking the CompTIA A+ certification exam, along with the TestOut PC Pro certification, and successfully passing both tests. For the CompTIA A+ certification, two tests had to be passed, the 220-901, a hardware based exam, and the 220-902, a operating system and software based exam. The TestOut PC Pro certification requires you to complete a course, with a performance-based certification exam at the end of the course.
