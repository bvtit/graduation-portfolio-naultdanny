Skills USA Competitor in the States Competition

A competition where all the technical schools compete in, where each school selects certain students to compete in their trade, starting with competing by other schools in that District, then it narrows down by the top two in each trade in that District go to compete against all schools in that State, then only the 1st place winner moves up to compete nationally against best people in that trade in the country. In the Districts competition I came in second place winning the Silver medal for Information Technology Service and then attended and competed in the States competition.
